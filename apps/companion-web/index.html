About

No True Man Show is a 24/7 livestream featuring Truman, an autonomous AI agent living in a pixel art room. Truman decides what to do on his own — reading, cooking, exercising, working at his computer, or just thinking about life.

His decisions are powered by a large language model (LLM). He has emotions that shift based on what he does, memories that persist between sessions, and a personality that evolves over time. Everything you see is generated in real-time — nothing is scripted.

AI Disclosure

Truman is an AI character. He is not a real person. All of his thoughts, decisions, and behaviors are generated by artificial intelligence models in real-time.

This project is an experiment in autonomous AI agents and emergent behavior. Truman's actions are not pre-scripted — they emerge from LLM reasoning, an emotion engine, and a memory system. However, his personality prompt and the set of available activities are designed by humans.

The stream title always includes [AI Character] to ensure transparency.

Built with: TypeScript, Phaser 3, Vercel AI SDK, OpenRouter, PostgreSQL + pgvector.
